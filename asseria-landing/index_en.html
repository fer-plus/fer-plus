About Asseria Center

A small hotel with a big story

Welcome to Asseria Center, a small hotel with many amenities built on the foundation of tradition and needs of contemporary man.

Our story is the result of love for the Benkovac area where our roots hail from. We also see a future there that we want to improve as much as possible, and make the whole area more prosperous.

By restoring and upgrading the old Asseria hotel in the center of Benkovac town, we are creating a new, positive story that connects all of hinterland’s bests. Just like the historic Asseria, the powerful Roman town we are named after, it’s our mission to provide a place where you can taste and experience the best of Zadar and Šibenik hinterlands.

The ideal place for an accessible vacation tailored to you

If you need a break from the everyday hustle, a vacation near the sea, yet far enough away from the crowds, or if you are travelling to Dalmatia for business, Asseria Center is the right choice for you.

We are one of the rare hotels that, throughout the year, offers a chance for a break and enjoying the taste, nature, and rich cultural-historical heritage of the Zadar hinterland.

Along with carefully furnished rooms with balconies and a view of the town and the Ravni Kotari plains, we offer you:

  • Spa and wellness zone with a pool
  • Hair and beauty salon
  • Delicacy store with authentic products
  • Conference room, working area, and meeting room
  • Restaurant offering traditional dishes and drinks
  • Tasting room with awarded wines and other amazing local products
  • Confectionery
  • Rooftop bar

Discover, taste, and relax

Situated between the luscious green plains of Ravni Kotari and the mountainous region of Bukovica, less than a half-hour drive to Zadar and around 40 minutes away from Šibenik, Asseria Center will allow you the ease of exploring Zadar and Šibenik hinterland.

And there is so much to explore!

History

Whether you prefer adventures in nature to a relaxed sightseeing of historical-cultural localities, or a visit to wineries and oil mills, the hinterland will satisfy all your cravings.

You just need to let go!
Cultural Heritage

Climb the hill above Benkovac to see the medieval fort Kaštel Benković and the Benkovac Heritage Museum located inside.

Not only is the amazing view of Ravni Kotari plains worth the climb, in the Museum there is a cannon constructed by Leonardo da Vinci himself!
Activities

If you enjoy a more active exploration of the hidden gems in new locations, you will like the newly established bike routes.

Explore the ancient Roman towns Nadin and Asseria, take a swim in the Karin Sea, find peace in the Franciscan monastery in Donji Karin, and enjoy the beauty of the Karišnica river.
Vrana Lake

Birdwatching is extremely useful for your physical and mental health. In Vransko Jezero nature park, not far from Asseria Center, you’ll fall in love with the activity.

Take a walk, breathe some fresh air in, and relax by the murmur of water from the Krka waterfalls.
Gastro

This area's wine and gastro scene is something we are particularly proud of.

Taste our rewarded wines, some world-quality honey, and ecological olive oils that'll tickle your taste buds.
